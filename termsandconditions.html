Only if both of you choose to continue afterwards do your profiles get revealed to each other and a private chat opens up.

1.5 Glimpse. Glimpse lets you record a short video (a few seconds) of yourself, optionally add a short caption, and post it to a recurring time "window" for an audience you choose: Hopeful (anonymous discovery), Matched, Friends, or Pledged. Videos and captions sent to the Hopeful or Matched audiences are checked by automated moderation before they appear, and are shown with an abstract glyph and a compatibility score — not your name or profile (though the video does show your appearance). Videos sent to Friends or a Pledged partner are shown with your name and photo to those people. Glimpse can also stitch a day's clips (yours and your friends'/partner's) into a short recap video you can save or share. Posting to Hopeful or Matched, and exporting a recap, may cost Credits or be included with Premium; Friends/Pledged posts are free (see Section 6).

1.6 Connections, Friends and Pledge. You can form ongoing connections with other users: a Match (after a mutual Mini-Date), a Friend (added with a personal friend code), and a Pledge (a mutual, exclusive commitment between matched users, formed with a match code). While you're pledged, some blind-date and matching features are turned off, and some features only work with your pledged partner. You can end any connection at any time.

1.7 Places (couple diary). Connected users (matched or pledged) can keep a shared diary of places they've been together — including place names and locations, dates, photos, captions and notes added by either person. Both members of the connection can see the diary. Posting a place may cost Credits or be included with Premium (see Section 6).

1.8 AI voice companion. The Service may let you have a live voice chat with an automated AI companion (not another person). It's time-limited and may cost Credits.

1.9 Ambassador mode. We may invite some users to an optional "ambassador" mode that helps seed the matching queue. If you turn it on, some of your calls may be flagged so they're excluded from certain metrics, and a call you start in this mode may not cost you Credits.
