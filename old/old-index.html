What we do

We turn hard infrastructure into products people love.

Most consumer apps stop at a pretty screen. We build the unglamorous engine underneath — real-time, safe, and built to bill — then wrap it in something worth opening.

Real-time voice infra

Sub-second, anonymous live audio at scale — media routing, matching queues, and the reliability a live product demands.

AI & trust-and-safety

Always-on moderation pipelines — live transcription, abuse detection, verification and liveness — so quality and protection stay high.

Payments-grade backends

Append-only ledgers, credits economies and store billing built to financial-services standards — auditable, exact, and trusted.

Mobile product, end-to-end

From first interaction model to shipped iOS & Android app — design, build, and the calm motion that makes it feel alive.

0→1 product building

We take an idea to a working, monetizing MVP — fast — then prove the loop before pouring on fuel.

SEA go-to-market

Localized UX, languages and local e-wallet rails, market by market — built for a mobile-first region of 680M+.

Who we are

A studio, not a shop.

Deployku is a Singapore venture studio. We don't take tickets — we build and own consumer products, carrying each one from a blank screen to a live, monetizing app.

Our edge is the hard part everyone else avoids: real-time infrastructure, always-on AI safety, and ledgers built to financial-services standards. It's the kind of engineering that takes years to get right — and it's exactly where we live.

We move like a startup and build like a bank. The result is products that are fast to ship, safe to use, and honest about the numbers — the foundation a serious company is built on.
